Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and resembles Earth in size and density. It is primarily composed of rocks and has a thick atmosphere made up of 599 carbon dioxide, causing a runaway greenhouse effect. Surface temperatures reach around 494 degrees Celsius, making it the hottest planet. Venus takes about 449 Earth days to orbit the Sun, while a day on Venus lasts about 403 Earth days. Due to its extreme conditions, Venus is not considered habitable. It has been explored by various missions such as Venera and Magellan.
